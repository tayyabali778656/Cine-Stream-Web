Privacy Policy

At CineStream, your privacy is our priority. We explicitly state that we collect minimal user data — primarily through cookies and analytics — to understand site traffic and improve your browsing experience.

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. These third-party vendors may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other sites on the internet. We do not sell or share personal information with third parties. By using our site, you consent to our privacy practices.

Cookies: We use session cookies and local storage to save your preferences such as your watchlist and recently viewed content. No personally identifiable information is stored on our servers.

Third-Party Services: We use TMDB (The Movie Database) for metadata. Their privacy policy applies to their services.

Contact: For any privacy-related questions, email us at contact@cinestream.watch.
